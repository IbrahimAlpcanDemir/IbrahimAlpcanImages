About Us


Secret of London is a project that has been created by the event designer Ibrahim Alpcan Demir, who studied tourism and events management with focus on immersive and hospitality.

Ibrahim Alpcan is a passionate event designer who came up with the idea of using authentic venues and turning these into a pop-up restaurant experience cooperating with guest chefs and Michelin star chefs from all around the world.

The concept is as the name already states a secret venue, which will be revealed 2 hours before the reservation. The menus vary depending on which guest chef is hosting the event.

Throughout the event many surprises will make our guests enjoy the evening and allow them to explore tastes and flavours that have not been experienced before.
